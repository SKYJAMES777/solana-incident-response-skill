**Observed facts:** signatures, slots, addresses, provider comparisons
**Hypotheses:** ranked and labeled

**Next decision:** owner, deadline, confirmation required
**Next update:** time or trigger

## Public statement

State only confirmed impact, safe user actions, and the next update time. Do not speculate about identity, publish a working exploit, request seed phrases, or send users to an unverified migration link.

## Disclosure and closure

Verify recipient and encrypted channel before sharing sensitive details. Send a non-mainnet reproduction, affected versions, impact, and mitigations. Coordinate publication when early detail increases harm. Preserve sent notices and acknowledgements.

The final report includes executive summary, UTC timeline, affected assets, detection, root cause with confidence, containment decisions, recovery, user impact, evidence manifest, control gaps, and assigned follow-ups. Separate incident-time facts from later conclusions.
